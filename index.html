Our Story

Where Passion Meets Craft

We believe silver hair is not something to hide — it is something to celebrate. Sophizo was born from a simple truth: women who embrace their natural colour deserve products that make it extraordinary.

Our hair serum is the key, formulated to banish yellow and brassy tones.

Our hair oil is the perfect finish— intended specifically for undyed hair.

And because great hair deserves a great frame, we suggest our eyebrow dye (brown not black). This product ensures your brows remain bold and defined but not scary!
